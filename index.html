The Founder's Story

Built From
Passion & Precision.

"I started Miniworks in 2018 with a single FDM printer, a rented garage, and an unshakeable belief that 3D printing would change how India creates, manufactures, and innovates. Seven years later, that belief has become an ecosystem."

What began as a personal obsession with additive manufacturing grew into India's most trusted 3D printer store. Every machine in our catalog has been run, calibrated and tested by our own engineers before it reaches yours. That is not a policy; it is a promise made on day one and kept every single day since.

We stock FDM, Resin, Industrial and Desktop printers — and we stand behind every single one with a full year of warranty, dedicated setup support, and a technical team available seven days a week.
