The Founder's Story

Built From
Passion & Precision.

"I started Miniworks in 2018 with a single FDM printer, a rented garage, and an unshakeable belief that 3D printing would change how India creates, manufactures, and innovates. Seven years later, that belief has become an ecosystem."

What began as a personal obsession with additive manufacturing grew into India's most trusted 3D printing destination. Every product in our catalog has been tested by our own hands before it reaches yours. That is not just a policy; it is a promise made on day one and kept every single day since.
