The Founder's Story

Born From
Passion & Precision.

"I started Miniworks in 2018 with a single FDM printer, a rented garage, and an unshakeable belief that 3D printing would change how India creates, manufactures, and innovates. 3DSelfiy was the moment that belief became something deeply personal."

3DSelfiy began as a bold experiment: could we turn every person into a work of art? The answer was yes. Using photogrammetry rigs and full-colour binder-jetting, we capture people in stunning detail and give them something they will treasure for generations.

What started as a single scanning booth at a Mumbai pop-up has grown into India's most-loved 3D selfie studio — chosen for weddings, product launches, school events, and corporate milestones across the country.
